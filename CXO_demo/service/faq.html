FAQ

  • Q: I've got an idea for a new feature how do I let you know?
    A: You'll find a thread in the Forum where you can let us know.
  • Q: I've found a bug, how do I let you know?
    A: You'll find a thread in the Forum where you can let us know.
  • Q: What have you got planned for the future?
    A: We have a few good ideas up our sleeves, and we want your feedback on them. We'll share this information with you via the Forums but please just let us know if there’s something that's important to you.
  • Q: Does Company have any rights on the software I develop and publish into service innovation center?
    A: You retain full rights over your own software at all times.
  • Q: My app supports multiple platforms, how do I publish them?
    A: If you have developed your app on multiple platforms (e.g. Windows Mobile and Symbian) then you will need to individually publish the app for each platform supported.
  • Q: Supported Platforms - I'm interested in a platform that's not listed?
    A: We had to come up with an initial set of platforms to build the cataloguing system. If you would like new categories added then let us know via the forums.
