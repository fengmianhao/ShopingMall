Create Service Easily!

On service innovation center, you can follow the steps below to develop your services:

If you want to develop a new service , You can register as a partner on the the service innovation center. It provides you with modules and APIs for developing new services.

If you are interested in certain modules or APIs, you can apply for subscribing to the modules, APIs, development tools, related development documents, and code samples at any time. After the administrator approves your application, you can download them.

After developing a new service, you can apply for testing the service on the service innovation center. After the administrator approves your application, you can test the service.

After a new service passes the test, you can apply for issuing the service on the service innovation center. After the administrator approves your application, the service will be displayed on the End-User Portal.
